About Us


With over six decades of rich heritage, Sen Group stands tall as a cornerstone of Eastern India's economic landscape, boasting an impressive annual turnover of 25 million USD. Renowned for our expertise in Poultry Feed, Breeding Farms, and Hatcheries, we've seamlessly expanded our reach into diverse sectors such as Animal & Aquaculture Feed, Agricultural Products & Biofertilizers, Vertical Integration of Poultry, Construction, Bakery, Retail Marketing, and IT solutions.



Chairman's message

A dedicated visionary and leader of the Sen Group, Mr. Samarendra Narayan Sen started his journey in the remote town of Joka, Kolkata as a backyard farmer back in the 1950s. Through his unparalleled vision and unwavering focus, he managed to build an empire known as the Sen Group leaving an indelible mark in the world of entrepreneurship and industry leadership.
